About Us

We are a family company founded in 1942 by Pawtucket Anderson Jamie III out of his front yard. He had a pashion to make the best gosh darn desserts and serve them to those he loved. We like to continue with that passion today by making sure we use nothing but all natural ingredients and flavors in our pasteries.
